About

Anybody is a hyper-flexible sans-serif font, inspired by the classic Impact font. Made in 2018 by Tyler Finck, Anybody is meant for, well, anybody to use. From memes to movie titles, Finck aimed to create a font with boundless applications. With its slightly rounded forms and exaggerated proportions, Anybody is designed to be eye-catching, readable, and fun, making it suitable for branding, headlines, and various other projects.

The font was born out of an affinity for Eurostile and a love of the 90s. With a whopping 18 different font types on Google Fonts, and many more on his website, the font caters to a wide range of uses, from playful applications like memes to serious projects. This versatility is reflected in the font's design, which combines bold geometric shapes with a touch of personality.

In addition to its broad appeal, Anybody is designed with readability in mind, making it suitable for both digital and print applications. Its slightly rounded edges and exaggerated proportions contribute to a friendly, approachable appearance, enhancing its usability in various contexts. By emphasizing flexibility and fun, Tyler Finck has successfully crafted a typeface that embodies the idea that it can be used by anybody, for anything.
